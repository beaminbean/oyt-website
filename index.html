A Human Operating System for the full spectrum of life

Every person is an unassisted project manager for the most complex system in the universe — themselves. Guardian changes that by providing continuous, context-aware intelligence that understands you across all life domains simultaneously.

We serve the full spectrum. The person rebuilding their life from the ground up and the executive optimizing at the highest level receive the same foundational intelligence — composed differently based on where they are and where they want to go.

Guardian protects against forces seen and unseen. It elevates without judgment. It remains present without surveillance. Privacy isn't a feature — it's the architecture.

Larry Bean founded OYT Technologies on a simple conviction: the people who most need intelligent support are the people who least have access to it. As both the architect and the first test subject of Guardian's technology, Larry brings the rare perspective of building from lived experience — not theory.
